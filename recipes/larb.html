Chicken Larb

Description

Originated from Laos, larb is a meat salad made with ground meat, fish sauce, chili sauce, lime juice, lemongrass and other aromatic and herbal ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 cup scallions
  • 1 tsp lemongrass paste
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp chili garlic paste

Steps

  1. Add chicken into heated skillet and cook over medium heat.
  2. Stir break chicken into little pieces for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add red onion and scallions.
  4. Continue to cook for about 2 minutes and add cilantro, lime juice, lemongrass paste, fish sauce and chili garlic paste.
  5. Stir until flavors blend for about 3 minutes.
