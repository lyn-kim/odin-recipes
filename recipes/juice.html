Green Juice

Description

Juices are a great way to get your daily dose of fruits and vegetables!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 mango - peeled, seeded and chopped
  • 3/4 cup fresh spinach

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add extra ingredients to taste.
  4. Enjoy!
