Overnight Chai Pomegranate Oats

Description

Overnight oats are a perfect grab-and-go breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Quaker Oats
  • 1 chai tea bag
  • 1/4 cup low-fat vanilla Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup low-fat milk
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate seeds

Steps

  1. Add Quaker Oats to a container of your choice and pour in Chai Tea Latte.
  2. Layer on yogurt and top with honey and pomegranate seeds.
  3. Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning!
